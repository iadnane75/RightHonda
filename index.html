Rick Case Honda · Davie, FL
DOUBLED FACTORY WARRANTY
The only dealer in the USA to double Honda's warranty.
EXCLUSIVE NATIONWIDE
How It Works
  • Honda's standard new-vehicle factory warranty is 3 years / 36,000 miles (bumper-to-bumper) and 5 years / 60,000 miles (powertrain)
  • Rick Case Honda doubles both figures — extending coverage to 6 years / 72,000 miles (bumper-to-bumper) and 10 years / 120,000 miles (powertrain) at no added cost
  • The extended coverage is honored at Rick Case Honda's own service center and is applied automatically at the time of purchase
  • No enrollment or paperwork required — it is included with every qualifying new Honda purchase
  • This program is underwritten by Rick Case Automotive Group itself, making it a dealer-backed extension — not a Honda factory extension
Best For
Long-term owners who plan to keep their Honda for 8–10 years; high-mileage drivers; anyone wanting maximum peace of mind on a major purchase.
vs. Right Honda: Right Honda offers no warranty extension. This is one of the most powerful purchase differentiators in the Honda dealer network nationally.

Rick Case Honda · Davie, FL
RICK CASE REWARDS PROGRAM
Earn points on every dollar spent — forever.
LOYALTY PROGRAM
How It Works
  • Every customer who purchases a vehicle receives a Rick Case Rewards Card at no charge
  • Points are earned on every transaction — sales, service, and parts — and accumulate across all Rick Case dealerships
  • Points can be redeemed on future vehicle purchases or service visits, saving customers hundreds or even thousands of dollars over time
  • Cardholders receive free car washes for life at any Rick Case location — simply show the card
  • Cardholders also receive discounted gasoline at participating partner stations
  • The card works across all 12 Rick Case dealerships covering Honda, Hyundai, Volkswagen, and more
Best For
Repeat customers, families who buy multiple vehicles, and service-loyal customers who want to earn on every oil change and repair.
vs. Right Honda: Right Honda has no equivalent loyalty points program. Rick Case Rewards creates strong retention — customers are financially incentivized to return for every service visit.

Norm Reeves Honda · Cerritos, CA (+ 6 locations)
PRICE PROTECTION GUARANTEE + 5-DAY EXCHANGE
Find it cheaper? We pay you. Don't love it? Swap it.
DUAL GUARANTEE
How It Works — Price Protection
  • After purchasing a new Honda from Norm Reeves, the customer has 5 full days to shop competing dealers
  • If the customer finds the identical vehicle (same year, model, trim, options) advertised for less at another dealer, Norm Reeves will pay the customer the difference
  • Alternatively, the customer may return the vehicle entirely within the 5-day window
  • Claim process: present the competing dealer's written, verifiable advertised price to Norm Reeves within the 5-day period
How It Works — 5-Day Exchange (Pre-Owned)
  • Pre-owned vehicle buyers have 5 calendar days from purchase/delivery to exchange the vehicle
  • Vehicle must be returned with no more than 500 additional miles and in the same condition as at purchase
  • Customer may swap for any other pre-owned vehicle in Norm Reeves' inventory
  • Delivery fees are non-refundable; all other purchase terms are reset for the new vehicle
How It Works — Internet Direct Pricing
  • Norm Reeves cross-checks every pre-owned vehicle price against hundreds of automotive websites daily
  • The result is a verified "Super Price" that eliminates the need to negotiate — the listed price is already market-optimized
Best For
Price-conscious shoppers who want assurance they got the best deal; first-time buyers nervous about overpaying; used car buyers who want a no-risk trial.
vs. Right Honda: Right Honda offers a basic price match; Norm Reeves goes further with a 5-day full-return option AND pays the customer if a cheaper price is found elsewhere.

Roush Honda · Westerville, OH
100% EMPLOYEE OWNERSHIP MODEL
Every person who serves you owns this dealership.
ESOP STRUCTURE
How It Works
  • Roush Honda operates as an Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP) — the dealership has been 100% employee-owned since 1997
  • Every full-time employee — from the general manager to the service technician to the detail team — is a direct co-owner of the business
  • Employees earn ownership shares as part of their compensation, which vest over time and grow in value as the dealership grows
  • Because every employee has a financial stake in the outcome, the culture produces exceptional accountability and customer care — the owner of the business is the person changing your oil
  • Management decisions are made with long-term employee and customer relationships in mind, not short-term corporate quotas
  • This structure also means no external shareholders or public market pressures — decisions are made by the people who work there
Best For
Customers who value authentic service relationships; anyone frustrated by the transactional feel of corporate dealerships; long-term Ohio residents who want a true community business partner.
vs. Right Honda: Right Honda is part of a $2B private group (Wilson Automotive). Roush's employee-ownership creates a fundamentally different accountability culture — it's the only model of its kind among the dealerships reviewed.

Buy once. Save forever. Shop inside the world's largest Honda showroom.
LIFETIME BENEFIT

  • If you find a better price, Lindsay will meet it — or your first service visit is free as compensation

  • Four service lanes and 42 service bays allow simultaneous high-volume throughput
Best For
Long-term owners who service their vehicle regularly; cost-conscious buyers who want predictable lifetime ownership costs; customers who prefer an immersive, unhurried shopping environment.
vs. Right Honda: Right Honda has no lifetime service benefit. Lindsay's oil change program is one of the strongest retention anchors of any dealer in this analysis.
